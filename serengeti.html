📅 When Is The Best Time To See The Great Migration?

The 2 million wildebeest move in a 1,800-mile loop. Here’s where they are each month:

Jan - Feb: CALVING SEASON 🐃
Location: Southern Serengeti / Ndutu Area
What Happens: 8,000 babies born DAILY. Predator paradise - lions, cheetahs, hyenas hunt non-stop.
Success Rate: 95% big cat sightings. Best for predator action photos.
Crowds: Medium. Book 3 months early.
Price: High season. $450/day.
Mar - May: LONG RAINS 🌧️
Location: Central to Western Serengeti
What Happens: Herds move north slowly. Roads muddy, some camps close. Lush green landscapes.
Success Rate: Good for scenery, harder for migration. Best birding time.
Crowds: Very low. Cheapest prices.
Price: Low season. $350/day.
Best For: Budget travelers, photographers who want green.
Jun - Jul: GRUMETI RIVER CROSSINGS 🐊
Location: Western Serengeti / Grumeti River
What Happens: First major river crossings. Crocodiles hunt. Herds moving to Mara.
Success Rate: 80% chance of crossing. Spectacular drama.
Crowds: High. Book 5 months early.
Price: Peak season. $500/day.
Aug - Sep: MARA RIVER - PEAK MIGRATION 🦓
Location: Northern Serengeti / Kenya Border
What Happens: THE famous Mara River crossings. 2 million animals cross. Most dramatic time on earth.
Success Rate: 90% if you stay 3+ days. Most expensive + crowded.
Crowds: PEAK. Only 200 vehicles/day allowed. Book 6-8 months early.
Price: Peak peak. $550/day.
Oct - Dec: RETURN SOUTH 🍃
Location: Central to Southern Serengeti
What Happens: Herds move back south for short rains. Spread out, less dramatic but still amazing.
Success Rate: Good. Less crowded than Aug-Sep.
Crowds: Medium. Good balance of price + wildlife.
Price: Shoulder season. $420/day.
