Serengeti National Park

Plan Your Safari

Serengeti National Park is one of the most famous national parks in Tanzania and in the world. It is located in northern Tanzania and is known for its wide plains, beautiful landscapes, and large number of wild animals.

The park is home to many animal species such as lions, elephants, giraffes, zebras, cheetahs, and buffaloes. Every year, millions of wildebeests and zebras move across the plains in what is called the Great Migration.

Serengeti National Park attracts many tourists from different countries. Visitors come to enjoy game drives, photography, and learning about wildlife conservation. Tourism in the park also supports the economy of Tanzania.

The Serengeti ecosystem is important because it protects animals and their natural habitats. conservation efforts help maintain the balance of nature and ensure future generations can enjoy this unique environment.
