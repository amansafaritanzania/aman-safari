Tarangire National Park Safari

Home to 3000+ elephants and ancient baobab trees. The best park in Tanzania for close elephant encounters and massive lion prides.

Why Tarangire?

Tarangire is only 2 hours from Arusha but feels like true wilderness. During dry season June to October, the Tarangire River is the only water source for miles. Thousands of elephants, zebras, and wildebeest come here to drink. You will see herds of 300+ elephants in one sighting.

Best Time to Visit: June to October is peak dry season. Animals gather at the river and game viewing is unbeatable. November to May is green season with fewer tourists, beautiful landscapes, and baby animals.

3000+ Elephants

Largest elephant population in Tanzania. See massive herds up close every single day.

Baobab Trees

Ancient baobabs over 1000 years old. Iconic African landscapes perfect for photos.

Big Cats

Lions, leopards, and cheetahs. Lions here climb trees just like Lake Manyara.

No Crowds

Less tourist traffic than Serengeti. More exclusive safari experience.

What to Expect on Your Tarangire Tour

7:30 AM: Pickup from Arusha hotel. Drive 2hrs to Tarangire gate.

9:30 AM - 4:00 PM: Full day game drive. We follow the Tarangire River where elephants, giraffes, and lions gather. Stop at Silale Swamp for lions and birds.

1:00 PM: Picnic lunch at one of the designated sites with baobab views.

4:30 PM: Exit park and drive back to Arusha. Arrive 6:30 PM.

Tip: Bring good binoculars. Tarangire has 550+ bird species, best birding park in Tanzania.
