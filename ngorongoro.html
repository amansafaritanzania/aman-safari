📅 Best Time to Visit Ngorongoro - Month by Month

Animals live here year-round, but weather + crowds change. Pick your month:

Jan - Feb: Peak Season, Best Weather ☀️
Weather: Dry, sunny, 15-25°C. Perfect game drives.
Animals: All Big 5 active. Calving season nearby in Ndutu = predators hunting.
Crowds: High. Book 6 months early.
Price: High season rates.
Mar - May: Green Season, Low Crowds 🌧️
Weather: Long rains. Muddy roads, cloudy mornings.
Animals: Still there but harder to spot in tall grass. Baby animals everywhere.
Crowds: Very low. You get the crater almost private.
Price: Low season discounts - save $50/day.
Jun - Oct: DRY SEASON, BEST GAME VIEWING 🦏
Weather: Cool, dry, 10-20°C. Clear skies, no rain.
Animals: PEAK. Animals crowd around lakes. 95% rhino sightings. Easy to spot lions.
Crowds: High but worth it. Book 4 months early.
Price: High season rates.
Nov - Dec: Short Rains, Good Value 🌦️
Weather: Short afternoon showers. Mornings clear.
Animals: Still excellent. Landscape turns green = amazing photos.
Crowds: Medium. Good balance.
Price: Shoulder season - save $30/day.

Why Ngorongoro Crater?

The Ngorongoro Crater is 600m deep and 20km wide. Over 25,000 animals live inside including the endangered black rhino. Because animals can't leave the crater, game viewing is guaranteed year-round. No other place in Africa lets you see lions, elephants, buffalo, leopards, and rhino in 6 hours.
