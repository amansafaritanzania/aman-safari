Paragraph 4: Which Route Should You Choose
Machame Route 6-7 days: Most scenic, called "Whiskey Route". Sleep in tents. 85% success rate. Best for fit hikers. Marangu Route 5-6 days: Only route with huts, called "Coca-Cola Route". Easiest but lowest success rate. Lemosho Route 7-8 days: Less crowded, best acclimatization, 96% success. This is what I recommend for first-time climbers. Rongai Route 6-7 days: Starts from Kenya side, driest route.

Paragraph 6: Best Time To Climb Kilimanjaro
January to March: Warmest months, clear mornings, fewer crowds. Great for photography. June to October: Dry season, coldest at summit but best overall weather. Busiest months - book 4 months early. November: Short rains begin, but climbable. April to May: Heavy rains - I don’t recommend climbing. We cancel and refund 100% if weather makes it unsafe. December: Good but can be wet and busy for Christmas.
