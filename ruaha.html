📅 Best Time to Visit Ruaha - Month by Month

Ruaha transforms completely between wet + dry season. Here’s the truth:

Jun - Oct: DRY SEASON - PEAK RUAHA 🦁
Weather: Bone dry, 25-35°C. Dusty but perfect game drives.
Animals: PEAK. Every animal drinks from Great Ruaha River. 10,000+ elephants, 25+ lion prides, wild dogs hunt daily. Best game viewing in Tanzania.
Crowds: Low. 90% fewer tourists than Serengeti. You get private sightings.
Price: High season. $400/day.
Best For: Serious safari lovers. Photographers. People who hate crowds.
Nov - May: WET/GREEN SEASON 🌧️
Weather: Rains Nov-Dec, Mar-May. Roads muddy, some impassable. Park turns lush green.
Animals: Dispersed. Elephants spread into bush. Harder to spot. BUT: Baby animals, 570+ bird species, stunning landscapes.
Crowds: Almost empty. Cheapest prices.
Price: Low season. $320/day.
Best For: Birders, photographers, budget travelers. NOT recommended for first safari.
