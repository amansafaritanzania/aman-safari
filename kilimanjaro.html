Day-by-Day Itinerary
Day 1 🏕️ Mandara Hut — Enter Kilimanjaro National Park and hike through montane forest 2,700 m Camp
Day 2 ⛺ Horombo Hut — Ascend through moorland with sweeping panoramic views 3,720 m Camp
Day 3 🌿 Acclimatize at Horombo or explore Mawenzi Ridge — rest day recommended 3,720 m Rest
Day 4 🏔️ Kibo Hut — Cross the alpine desert saddle to base camp 4,703 m Camp
Day 5 🌄 Summit & Descend — Midnight push to Uhuru Peak, then back to Horombo 5,895 m Summit!
Day 6 🚪 Descent to Marangu Gate — collect your summit certificate 1,860 m Exit
