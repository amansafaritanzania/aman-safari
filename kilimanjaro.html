About Us

Two Decades of Excellence

For over 20 years, Kilimanjaro Luxury Expeditions has set the standard for premium adventure travel in Tanzania. What began as a passion project by veteran mountain guides has evolved into the region's most trusted luxury outfitter, serving discerning travelers from around the globe.

We believe luxury and wilderness are not mutually exclusive. Our expeditions combine the raw beauty of Africa with white-glove service: gourmet chefs, private bathroom facilities, satellite communications, and comprehensive medical support at every camp.

Our commitment extends beyond our guests. We maintain the highest guide-to-client ratios in the industry, pay leading wages to our local team, and contribute 5% of profits to conservation and community development in the Kilimanjaro region.
